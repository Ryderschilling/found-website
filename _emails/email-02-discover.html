Feature spotlight

The people near you
are already here.

The Discover tab shows you real Christians in your area — matched by life stage, shared interests, and how close they are to you. Not an algorithm trying to keep you scrolling. Just people who actually line up with who you are.

Two ways to reach out
Connect
Tap Connect on someone's card to send a connection request. When they connect back, it's mutual — and from there, you can message them directly.
Wave
Not ready to connect yet? Send a wave — a lighter signal that you noticed someone. Lower stakes, still meaningful.

Each person has a match score based on what you have in common — shared interests, life stage, proximity. The closer the match, the higher they appear. It's not random.

You can also adjust the location filter to search beyond your neighborhood or narrow it down to just your city. Tap the filter pill at the top of Discover to change it.
