Finding people is the start. But real community happens when you actually show up somewhere together — and when conversations go deeper than a profile card.

Groups

Groups are where consistent community lives. Find one built around something you care about — a weekly run, a small group, a book club — or start your own.

Tap Groups in the tab bar. Browse what's near you, or tap + to create one. Give it a name, a description, a meeting schedule, and your city — and it becomes visible to nearby people who share the interest.

Messaging

Once you and someone have mutually connected, the conversation is open. No waiting, no barriers.

Tap the Messages tab to see your threads, or start a new one from a connection's profile. Keep it casual, make a plan, or just say hi. The first message is always the hardest — but it's worth it.

Community rarely finds you. Most of the time, you have to go first. FOUND just makes it easier to take that step.
