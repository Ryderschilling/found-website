People in FOUND are putting together real get-togethers nearby — Saturday morning hikes, weekly Bible studies, dinners for young couples. You can host one too, or just show up to one that's already happening.

How to create an event
01 Open FOUND and tap the Events tab at the bottom of the screen.
02 Tap + to create a new event. Add a name, date, time, location, and a quick description of what it is.
03 Your event is shared with your connections and people nearby. They can see who's going before they commit.

You can also browse events near you, filter by date or type, and RSVP in one tap. No group chats to manage, no coordination headache — just show up.

Real community forms around real moments. Events are how FOUND moves from your screen to your actual life.
