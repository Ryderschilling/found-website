Welcome

We all need people to run with.

FOUND helps you discover meaningful community by connecting you with people nearby who share your interests, life stage, and desire for deeper relationships.

How it works

01

Create Your Profile

Add a photo, write a short bio, select your interests, and create your Highlight Reel. The more complete your profile, the easier it is for others to get to know you before you meet.

02

Discover People

Explore people in your area who share similar interests, values, or life experiences. Whether you're new in town or simply looking to expand your community, there's someone waiting to meet you.

03

Connect

Send a connection request and start a conversation. Ask a question, share a common interest, or simply introduce yourself. Great friendships often begin with a simple message.

04

Meet Up

Take the next step. Grab coffee, go for a walk, catch some waves, invite someone to dinner, or join a local event. Community grows when people move beyond screens and into real life.

05

Do Life Together

The goal isn't more followers — it's meaningful relationships. Build a community where people know you, encourage you, celebrate with you, and walk alongside you through life's highs and lows.
